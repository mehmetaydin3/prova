# Trust & Conversion Model

This document defines the strategic approach to reducing hiring uncertainty and optimizing the marketplace booking funnel.

## 1. The Trust Equation
Trust in a creative marketplace is built through the combination of **Verified Proof** and **Behavioral Consistency**.

| Component | Signal | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Identity** | Verified Profile Badge | Authenticates the individual or studio. |
| **Craft** | Media Portfolio | Direct proof of technical and creative skill. |
| **Authority** | Credentials & Badges | External validation (e.g., "Grammy Winner", "Berklee Grad"). |
| **Reliability** | Response Time & Job Count | Predictive indicator of service quality and availability. |
| **Social Proof**| Ratings & Reviews | Validates experience from the buyer's perspective. |

---

## 2. Strategic Placement of Signals

### A. Discovery Layer (PLP)
*   **Goal:** Instill immediate confidence for comparison.
*   **Required Signals:** Star Rating (Count), Response Time indicator, and Top Pro/Verified badge.

### B. Consideration Layer (PDP)
*   **Goal:** Build deep authority and remove specific hire-anxiety.
*   **Required Signals:** Detailed reviews with buyer names, verified audio/video samples, and a clear list of professional credentials.

---

## 3. Behavioral Performance Markers
High-performing creators are incentivized to maintain these signals:
*   **Response Rate:** Percentage of inquiries answered within 24 hours.
*   **Repeat Client Rate:** Indicator of long-term value and reliability.
*   **Profile Completeness:** Essential for algorithm visibility (100% completion required for "Top Pro" status).

---

## 4. Conversion Optimization Rules
To maximize "Search to Sale" conversion:
1.  **Eliminate Ambiguity:** Every service must have fixed tiers (Basic/Standard/Premium) with explicit deliverables.
2.  **Frictionless Entry:** Always provide a "Message Musician" option alongside "Book" for custom scope discussions.
3.  **Transparent Logistics:** Display turnaround times and revision counts upfront.
4.  **Sticky CTAs:** Keep the primary transaction action visible at all times during the PDP scrolling experience.
